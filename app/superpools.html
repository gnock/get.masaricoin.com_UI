Welcome Superpools Masari Miners! 

We have worked with @Dani to make the transition from Superpools to Masaricoin as seamless as possible.  Some things may look a bit different, but we have many similarities.  Masaricoin contributes the same amount to the Masari Devs as Superpools did.  Like Superpools, Masaricoin is trusted by the Devs, and the Masari community. 

Masaricoin is dedicated to Masari.  We do not mine any other coins.  We have 4 pool operators geo-located so support is available almost 24/7.  The Masaricoin discord community has grown considerably and if a pool operator doesn’t answer quickly, it’s likely someone else will offer help.  Also, one of our pool operators is on the Core Dev team! 

Some new things….  Masaricoin is a different from Superpools in the following ways:

  • PPLNS Payment System – This feature discourages pool hopping and rewards loyal miners
  • Email Alerts – Your worker goes down, you get an email letting you know
  • Sub-Address Support – You can mine with Sub-Addresses
  • Geo-Servers – We have servers located in Singapore, Germany, and the US.  Using pool.masaricoin.com will route you to the nearest node for less latency.
  • Customizable payout threshold and lower minimum payout level – Set your payout threshold to anywhere you like above 1 MSR.  Keep in mind, we eat the fees if you are equal to or greater than 20 MSR.
  • Config Generators – Sometimes these config changes get frustrating.  Just stop by our config generator and pull the most up to date configs!
  • Discord – We have a very active Discord community.  We are implementing a tip/lottery bot at the fork.  Very cool stuff, come check it out..

Change is sometimes hard, but we at Masaricoin are prepared to help in anyway possible to make things as smooth as possible.
